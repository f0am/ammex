Vision

Maintain sustainable financial and material management system support costs and reduce ongoing costs of supporting financial and material management systems. Establish core support services for the GCfm solution, extract better information, standardize and improve service levels through a service level agreement (SLA).


In-service support services

The service includes a single point of contact, in the form of a help desk to help desk approach through the GCfm Service desk. The following, but not limited to, support functions will be provided by the In-service support team:

• General inquiries;

• Incident management;

• Problem management;

• Master data management;

• Training services.

Functional application support services

Functional application support services are provided by the functional analysts and fall primarily in the application development and maintenance category. While continuously adhering to the principle of “intended use” of the SAP product, the functional analysts are responsible for:

• Configuring and maintaining the GCfm Solution;

• Defining the business requirements; and

• Leading the process design/enhancements and system requirements documentation.

By providing the following services:

• System management services;

• Configurable master data maintenance;

• Reporting services;

• Request escalation support (system monitoring and troubleshooting);

• Change request management; and

• Process execution.

Technical application support services

Technical application support services are provided by technical analysts and fall within the application development and maintenance, production and operations computing, and IT security services categories. Technical analysts are responsible for the implementation, the monitoring, and the maintenance of IT systems, by providing the following services:

• Configurable master data implementation;

• Infrastructure management;

• System monitoring and troubleshooting;

• Security management;

• Problem resolution to the technical environment;

• Environment patching;

• Performing transports and refreshes;

• Maintaining GCfm Solution technical documentation;

• SAP development (e.g. ABAP); and

• SAP BASIS.
